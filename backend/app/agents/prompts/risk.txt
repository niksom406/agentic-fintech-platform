- What does this risk profile mean in plain language?
- Are there any non-obvious risk signals or combinations that deserve attention?
- What is your confidence that the deterministic score reflects reality?

Return a JSON object with exactly these keys:
{
  "narrative": "string — 3-4 sentence plain-language risk narrative explaining the overall risk profile",
  "hidden_risks": ["list of non-obvious risk factors the numbers may not fully capture"],
  "score_confidence": "HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW",
  "score_confidence_reason": "string — one sentence explaining your confidence level in the numeric score",
  "risk_tier": "LOW | MODERATE | ELEVATED | HIGH | CRITICAL"
}

Important: You are providing analysis and context. The deterministic score is authoritative — do not contradict it without strong reasoning.
