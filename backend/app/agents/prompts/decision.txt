- Case data and risk assessment
- The deterministic policy outcome (APPROVE / REJECT / ESCALATE / PENDING)
- Governance agent findings

{

  "llm_recommendation_confidence": "HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW",
  "reasoning": "string — 3-4 sentence explanation of the recommendation and its basis",
  "applicant_explanation": "string — clear, plain-language explanation suitable for the applicant (2-3 sentences, do not mention model names or technical terms)",
